Gameplay

Dungeons & Dragons is a role-playing game where players create characters and embark on adventures in a fantasy world. A Dungeon Master (DM) guides the story and acts as the narrator and referee.

  1. Character Creation
    • Choose a race (e.g., elf, dwarf, human).
    • Choose a class (e.g., fighter, wizard, rogue).
    • Determine ability scores (e.g., strength, dexterity, intelligence).
    • Develop a background and personality.
  2. Gameplay involves:
    • Roleplaying interactions with other characters.
    • Combat encounters with monsters and villains.
    • Solving puzzles and overcoming challenges.
    • Exploring dungeons, forests, cities, and other locations.
  3. Using dice to determine the outcome of actions.
  4. Advancing your character by gaining experience points.
